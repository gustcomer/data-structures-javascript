Introduction

  The AVL Tree is basically a self-balancing binary search tree.

Definitions

- Left, Right, Left-Right, Right-Left rotations: the rotations made after each
insertion in order to keep the tree balanced
- Balance Factor: the height difference between the Right Subtree and the Left
Subtree of any Node

Invariants

- The heights of the two child subtrees of any node differ by at most one.
